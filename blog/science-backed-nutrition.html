Gut Health Research

The Microbiome-Diet Connection

Recent studies suggest that dietary diversity significantly impacts gut bacteria composition. Key findings include:

  • High-fiber diets correlate with greater microbial diversity (Nature, 2023)
  • Fermented foods may help maintain gut barrier function (Cell, 2022)
  • Artificial sweeteners show mixed effects in clinical trials

Practical Tip: Aim for 30+ different plant foods weekly to support microbial diversity.
